Innovation Pipeline Journey

A structured, six-stage process from raw idea to market-ready product

1

Student Idea Submission

Innovation Entry Point

Students and youth innovators submit their creative solutions to real-world problems.

  • Problem identification and description
  • Domain selection (agriculture, health, manufacturing, etc.)
  • Proposed solution concept and approach
  • Initial vision for impact
2

AI Industry Matching

Need Alignment Stage

Advanced AI analyzes your idea and matches it with real industry challenges.

  • Keyword and domain analysis using NLP
  • Database search of industry challenges
  • Smart matching to real-world needs
  • Transform from idea to problem-driven solution
3

Virtual Lab Validation

Feasibility Check Stage

Digital prototyping and system-level validation before physical implementation.

  • Component requirements analysis
  • System workflow logic verification
  • Basic feasibility assessment
  • Cost and complexity estimation
4

Mentorship & Funding

Development Stage

Connect with industry experts, mentors, and funding opportunities.

  • Domain experts and professors
  • Industry mentors and advisors
  • Incubator partnerships
  • Funding and grant opportunities
5

Patent Filing

Intellectual Property Stage

Protect your innovation as intellectual property and build technological sovereignty.

  • Patent category suggestion
  • Drafting guidance and support
  • Filing pathway assistance
  • IP protection and ownership
6

Industry Adoption / Startup

Commercialization Stage

Transform validated innovation into market-ready products and economic impact.

  • Industry licensing and adoption
  • Startup creation support
  • Market entry strategy
  • Job generation and value creation

    Patent Filing Pathway
    1

    Provisional Patent Application

    File immediately to secure your filing date. Valid for 12 months.

    Cost: Rs. 1,600 (individuals)  |  Timeline: 2–3 days
    2

    Complete Specification

    Submit detailed patent application within 12 months of provisional filing.

    Cost: Rs. 8,000 (individuals)  |  Timeline: Within 12 months
    3

    Examination Request

    Request examination of your patent application.

    Cost: Rs. 8,000 (individuals)  |  Timeline: Within 48 months
    4

    Patent Grant

    Receive patent certificate after successful examination.

    Timeline: 2–3 years from filing  |  Protection: 20 years
